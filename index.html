How the daily spin experience works

  1. 1. Editorial briefing

    Every spin page begins with a short-form article. Editors explain the day’s theme, such as “budgeting for travel” or “healthy productivity habits”, and link to longer learning modules. This ensures players gain practical advice before interacting with any ad inventory.

  2. 2. Consent-first activation

    The system waits until the visitor accepts analytics and advertising cookies. Once consent is logged, the AdSense script is initialised and rewarded ad formats can be prepared. We disable autoplaying creatives and label all incentives as advertisements when they originate from third parties.

  3. 3. Controlled spin

    Players trigger a “prepare” step that requests a rewarded placement or displays contextual tips. After the countdown, the spin button becomes active and the wheel uses weighted probabilities to determine the reward. Cooling periods stop repeat spins until the next eligibility window.

  4. 4. Follow-up missions

    Win or lose, visitors receive tailored recommendations: articles to read, webinars to attend, or partner offers to explore. This delivers genuine utility and keeps the session rooted in rich, policy-friendly content.

Monetisation standards and ad placement strategy

Context comes first

Ads are embedded only after a substantial block of editorial copy, video explainers, or interactive lessons. We avoid infinite scroll layouts that surface blank slots and instead curate focused, thematic pages with a clear beginning, middle, and end.

Rewarded ads with disclosure

When offering rewarded ad experiences, labels such as “Advertisement – watch to unlock a spin” are required. Our template ensures the disclosure is announced to assistive technologies and presented before any play button becomes active.

Continuous optimisation

Weekly audits review viewability, latency, and policy changes. If an ad network or campaign fails to meet the criteria, it is paused and replaced with house content or learning modules so that users never encounter empty, low-value screens.

Frequently asked questions

How much content do I need around the spin wheel?

We recommend at least 600 words of original, topic-focused text plus supplementary media such as infographics or short videos. Every spin page should feel like a mini guide, not a thin landing page.

Can I run the spin on mobile web and apps?

Yes. The same configuration applies to responsive web builds, progressive web apps, and wrapped hybrid apps. Ensure you follow the AdMob rewarded formats policy when embedding inside an application shell.

What data is stored about my players?

Only pseudonymous identifiers, consent choices, and reward histories are stored. Personally identifiable information remains optional and, when collected for fulfilment, is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms with strict retention periods.

How do you keep spam or abuse under control?

Automated checks look for suspicious spin patterns, device spoofing, and rapid account creation. Suspicious sessions trigger extra verification steps or manual review before high-value rewards are dispatched.

Do you provide creative assets for the ads?

We supply sizing guides and brand-safe colour palettes. Actual creatives should be delivered by your ad operations team or trusted partners so that policy compliance is maintained across every campaign.
