Privacy Notice

We believe great promotional experiences respect people’s data choices. This Privacy Notice explains the types of information Spin Rewards Hub collects, how it is processed, and the controls you have over your personal data.

Last updated: 5 June 2024

Information we collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to deliver the Spin Rewards Hub services and to comply with Google AdSense programme policies. Depending on how you interact with the site, this may include:

  • Contact information that you voluntarily submit through support or consultation requests.
  • Pseudonymous identifiers such as browser cookies, device attributes, and session IDs to maintain spin history.
  • Analytics events describing page views, button clicks, and content engagement so we can improve our articles.
  • Consent records documenting when you accepted, rejected, or updated cookie choices.

How we use information

Information is used to deliver the requested service and to ensure the integrity of the rewards programme. In practice this means:

  • Running the spin experience, managing eligibility windows, and preventing abuse or duplicate entries.
  • Responding to support enquiries, consultation requests, and compliance audits.
  • Improving the quality of editorial content by analysing aggregate engagement trends.
  • Measuring ad performance so that we can maintain an ad experience aligned with Google’s policies.

Legal bases for processing

We rely on different legal bases depending on the data type and jurisdiction. These include consent for analytics and advertising cookies, legitimate interest when we monitor abuse prevention signals, and contractual necessity when you request consultancy or fulfilment support. Where required, we seek explicit consent before processing sensitive data or transferring information outside your region.

Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies help us understand which resources are most helpful and allow AdSense to deliver relevant ads. You can reject non-essential cookies at any time using the consent banner or by adjusting your browser settings. We honour browser-level “Do Not Track” signals by limiting analytics collection and disabling personalised ads when the signal is present.

When third-party partners provide measurement pixels or rewarded ad placements, they are contractually required to respect our cookie policies and to only process data needed to provide their service.

Advertising and AdSense

Spin Rewards Hub uses Google AdSense to monetise certain content areas. Ads load only after visitors grant consent, and all ad calls follow Google’s policies for inventory quality, prohibited content, and user privacy. We do not permit the serving of ads on pages that lack substantive editorial material or that are under construction.

Google may use cookies and device identifiers to personalise ads. You can learn more about how Google handles data by visiting the Google Ads Privacy & Terms site. Visitors in the EEA and UK can access additional granular controls via the consent banner.

Data retention and security

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to deliver the service or to meet legal requirements. Spin activity logs are retained for up to 18 months, after which they are aggregated or deleted. Contact records are kept while your support ticket remains open. We use encryption in transit and at rest, limit access to authorised personnel, and conduct regular penetration tests to validate our safeguards.

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. You can exercise these rights by emailing privacy@spinrewardshub.com and providing sufficient information for us to verify your identity. We respond to all valid requests within one month unless a shorter timeframe is mandated by law.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. We encourage you to contact us first so we can address any concerns directly.

International data transfers

Our infrastructure partners may process data in the United States, the European Union, or other regions where they operate. When data leaves its origin country, we apply safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and conduct risk assessments on the recipient vendor.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@spinrewardshub.com or write to Spin Rewards Hub, 221B Market Street, Suite 502, Dublin, Ireland.
